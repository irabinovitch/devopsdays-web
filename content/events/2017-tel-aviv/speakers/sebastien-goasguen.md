## Keynote Speaker

Sebastien Goasguen is senior director of cloud technologies at Bitnami, where he leads all the Kubernetes efforts. Sebastien joined Bitnami through the acquisition of his startup Skippbox. Sebastien is a 20-year open source veteran. A member of the Apache Software Foundation, he worked on Apache CloudStack and Libcloud for several years before diving into the container world. He is an avid blogger and enjoys spreading the word about new cutting-edge technologies. He also trains developers and sysadmins on all things Docker and Kubernetes.
